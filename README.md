# Carrot Liberation
A 2D side scrolling game where you control a rabbit, equipped with an egg launcher fighting against the evil farmer
who has infected the carrot farms of the rabbit village and turned them into monsters. 
It is up to the player to fight against hordes of monster carrots and other traps created by the evil
farmer to ensure safety of the rabbit village.

[Latest Build](https://lisguo.github.io/Carrot_Liberation.jar)

#Story
This is the story of a bunny who lived near a farm which had delicious carrots. The bunny would always sneak over the the farm
and steal those delicious carrots for the bunny community. One day, the farmer was fed up with it and cooked up a pesticide
to get rid of the mischievous rabbit. It all went wrong and soon everything in the land was
infected. The rabbit grew as tall as a bear and started to speak, the carrots received faces and 
formed appendages, and the farmer turned outright evil! Bring in those carrots while you can, soldier!

#Gameplay
- Story Mode
  - Fight and defeat the evil farmer who lies at the end of the farm.

- Survival Mode
  - See how long you can last as you fight through waves of monsters and traps
as well as poisonous carrots that stand in your way. This mode is an endless fight through many levels.
Those who choose this level are in for a challenge.
